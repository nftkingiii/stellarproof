From private invoices to a verifiable Stellar attestation.

The browser demo is instant. The real cryptographic proof runs asynchronously on Linux, then becomes visible to the web app as a published proof artifact.

01

Submit

Enter invoice amounts, age, debtor rating, and applicant account in the proof lab.

02

Prove

RISC Zero executes the underwriting policy off-chain and emits a public journal.

03

Attest

A Soroban contract verifies the receipt through the RISC Zero verifier router and stores the result.

Input
